BIOGRAPHY

Before starting Alexandra & Associates, Alexandra was senior consultant and corporate coach at Vitalwork, a Rochester based consulting company.  She designed and developed a one-on-one leadership program that she delivered to over 600 people in one organization alone, facilitated hundreds of Team Building Programs, and worked with Executives of many industries to support the development of self-confident, self-expressed and productive leaders.

Alexandra graduated from McGill University in Education.  Her many certifications include, Image Developer, Life Coach, Professional Behavioral Analyst (CPBA), Professional Values Analyst  (CPVA), with certifications to deliver Path of Dialogue and Personal Vitality Trainings.  She studied with One Spirit, an Interfaith Seminary in New York, and is an ordained Interfaith Minister.  She is a member of the International Coaching Federation, Executive Women of Palm Beaches, and is a member of the Board of The Counseling Ministry of South Florida.

As a public speaker, formerly on the Board of the Public Speakers Association of Ontario, trainer and coach, Alexandra’s clients have included groups and individuals from a diverse set of backgrounds and industries.  She has worked with the Ontario Government, the Catholic School Board of Toronto, The Roman Catholic Diocese of Rochester, NY, Real Estate, Medicine, Insurance, Law, Banking, Pharmaceuticals, Technology, Sales and Service, Telecommunications, Higher Education to name a few.
