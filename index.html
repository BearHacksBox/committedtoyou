Professional Coaching is a specialized relationship that helps people produce the results they desire and deserve in their lives. Through the process of coaching, clients deepen their learning, improve their performance, and enhance their quality of life.

Confidence Coaching is designed to support people in discovering their personal power and recognizing their true genius. With the use of the Alexandra Approach™ to replace self-limiting beliefs with empowering points of view, they will have the confidence they need to attain the happiness and success they want.

The Client is the true expert in his/her life. Every client is unique, creative, resourceful, and whole.
My responsibility is to:

 
  • Discover, clarify, and align with what the client wants to achieve
  • Encourage client self-discovery
  • Challenge limiting beliefs
  • Elicit client-generated solutions and strategies
  • Hold the client responsible and accountable
